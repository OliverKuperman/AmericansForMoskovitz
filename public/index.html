Why Dustin Moskovitz

A Leader Built for This Moment

Moskovitz combines proven executive leadership, extraordinary generosity, and unmatched foresight on the issues that define our era.

Proven Leadership

As co-founder and CTO of Facebook and later CEO of Asana, Moskovitz built billion-dollar enterprises from the ground up, coding on the frontlines alongside his teams, not issuing orders from afar.

Transformative Philanthropy

Through his foundation Coefficient Giving, Moskovitz has directed billions of his own dollars to fighting malaria, reducing lead exposure, and expanding access to clean water, and has pledged to give nearly all of his wealth away.

Forward-Thinking Vision

Moskovitz recognised the dangers of unregulated AI years before it became a national conversation. He also champions housing reform and supports Democratic candidates who share his values of civil, productive governance.
